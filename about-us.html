BACKGROUND

Delta International Film and Cultural Festival is a cultural concert, which tends to bring youths, children, and elders alike together in one arena for the single purpose of uplitiing and celebrating our cultural heritage. This event promises to bring together various cultural heritage of the diverse people of Delta state into Asaba the administrative capital of Delta state.

The youths from Delta State who form the greater percentage of the membership of the Nollywood movie industry have been exposed to various cultural activities and teachings by visiting men and women who are well knowledgeable in the cultural practices of most countries in Africa and the lives and careers of most of the youths have taken turns for the betier through this exposure and lectures. Delta State Asaba in particular has become the center of Nollywood litile wonder some have coined the name Asaba Wood to show how central the state has become in the movie industry.

BENEFITS FOR THE YOUTHS AND STATE

  • Spiritual uplitiing and edification.
  • Funds will be generated for promoting young talents.
  • Development of talent in the art of film making in the Delta region to an enviable height.
  • Empowerment and engagement for the youths of our states and the Delta region. Career development.
  • Professional and leadership development.

Through this film festival, we will expose the diverse cultural beauty of the Delta people and we will work hard in the preservation of our cultural heritage. The benefits of cultural film festivals should not only be known by practioners in Nollywood this is the main reason we decided to hold a cultural film festival that will take the youths of our dear state back to understanding our state's cultural heritage and to protect these cultures for posterity.

For this median edition, during the course of this festival and concert, we have decided to expand the event and encourage some talented core cultural African filmmakers and entertainers who ordinarily May not have the financial capability to produce their cultural films Through a talent hunt to be part of this year's Film festival program.

OUR GOAL

The main goal of this film festival is to provide an avenue for our filmmakers to showcase their ideas and talents, to create a place for our people to come and enjoy and celebrate the rich cultural displays of most of the Nigerian states that will atiend the festival. The festival's main goal is to empower Delta and Nigeria youths by identifying talents skilled in the art of African folklores and traditional dance performances, It will provide thousands of youths with their ultimate dream the innate opportunity to exhibit their skills for the chance to become professional traditional entertainers. For the investors involved, this film festival which will feature great filmmakers and artists of our time is an exciting opportunity for the selected few to reach their clients and consumers of their products. Film and cultural dance is a life-changing smart route to atiracting greater audience for patronage and market opportunities. Every year, we intend to assess hundreds of talented candidates from all over the Delta State but at the moment we are taking off with selecting 50 or more promising talents to showcase their filmmaking and storytelling talents.

OUR STANDARD

Our standard is founded and operated in line with the rules, regulations and best practices given by local and international Film festivals and concerts and standard rules for talents hunts. We operate with the approval and endorsement of the Corporate affaires commission, the Marketer's and filmmakers in Africa, Association of various African film practioners. Most importantly, we want to engage the people by offering quality and rich African language films and entertainment contents, offering betier entertainment to the Nigerian peoples. The proposed Film festival and concert has the capacity and potential when fully explored to provide Delta thespians with the opportunity to develop her cultural heritage and encourage her younger generation to embrace cultural development opportunities.

To bring to Delta State a high level film festival which will be culturally based with the intention of focusing the youth to embracing our cultural diversity and understanding the reasons for our unity through these cultural diversity. To celebrate the diversity and creativity of international cinema with cultural values.
